STORY

CONVO began with a simple question: what if the living room was arranged for people, not screens? The Convo is our answer — a single sofa, made to commission, designed to pull a room into conversation.

What we believe

01

Conversation first

Every curve, every seat depth, every armrest angle exists to make eye contact easier and conversation longer.

02

One sofa, made for you

We build a single model, in your fabric, your configuration, your dimensions. No seasonal line-up. No compromise.

03

Made to outlive us

A hardwood frame carries a lifetime warranty. Covers unzip for re-upholstery decades from now.

"

A room should gather people. A sofa should help.
Everything we make starts from that.

— The CONVO studio
