Details

A sofa designed
around conversation

The Convo is a modular, curved sofa engineered to bring people face-to-face. Its geometry — a shallow arc softened by deep seats — naturally gathers guests, replacing the screen-facing line-up of the typical living room with something more human.

Hand-stitched in our Lisbon atelier on a hardwood frame with eight-way hand-tied springs, every Convo is made to a single commission.
